Dr. Bali Pulendran lab, Emory Vaccine Center, Emory University, Atlanta, GA USA

I am a bioinformatics analyst with a goal in exploring immune, inflammatory and metabolic alterations during viral infections with relevance to human health. In particular, I am interested in the identification of key regulatory genes and/or pathways which may explain vaccine induced protective/perturbed immunity.

Keywords: Bioinformatics, Systems Biology, Vaccinology, Transcriptomics, Metabolomics, Lipidomics, Java, MySQL, R Programming, HIV-1 and ART, BigData, Data Science
